Naluri

Naluri is a digital health platform that provides corporate wellness programs aimed at improving employee health and productivity. This programme includes workshops, webinars and one-on-one coaching sessions. It will help employees to manage their mental health, physical and financial matters.

Paywatch

Paywatch

Paywatch is designed to provide financial assistance for employees, offering them greater control over their earnings and helping them achieve better financial stability. By this, employees can withdraw a portion of their wages before their scheduled payday. It will help to reduce financial stress, increase job loyalty and satisfaction & retention.
